Damian's Top 3 movies of the recent years

These are my top 3 movies this decade (so far)

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse

A really artistic movie with a very interesting twist that questions comic book movies as a medium in a very clever way.

The Batman

A dark, grounded Batman movie with a very convincing Robert Pattinson as the Dark Knight.

Soul

This movie is a must watch for every person of every age, but especially recommended to people that like to ask the big questions about life.
